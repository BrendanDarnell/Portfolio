Projects

  • Lemme Know

    Lemme Know helps people stay safe while enjoying activities they love. Users create events for upcoming activities, providing some details about what they are doing, when they are expected to return, and a contact's phone number. If they do no return from the event by the specified time, their contact is sent a text message asking to check up on the user.

  • Maintain My Ride

    Maintain My Ride is an app that provides do-it-yourselfers a place to record maintenance records for their vehicles. After creating an account, users can add all of their vehicles and then record important maintenance information such as the vehicle's mileage, the type of maintenance done, the date, and notes.

  • Hike My National Parks

    Hike My National Parks saves users time when planning a hike to one of America's national parks by providing important information, which would usually require visiting multiple web pages. When a user enters a national park into the search bar, the app displays the park entrance fee, the current weather, and a list of trails with images and useful details.
