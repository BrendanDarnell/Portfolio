After studying chemistry in college and working in various scientific industries, I realized that I had yet to find a career that I was passionate about. I decided to take some computer science classes and it was love at first byte when I took an intro to programming course. This led me to enroll in Thinkful's full-stack web-development program. I love creating web applications with end-user purpose and functionality at its core, while striving to write code that is easy to read and reusable.

In my free time you'll most likely find me in the mountains, at the dog park, riding my motorcycle, brewing beer, or reading a book.
